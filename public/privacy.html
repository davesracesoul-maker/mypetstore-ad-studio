Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

MyPetStore Ad Studio ("the app", hosted at mypetstore-ad-studio.netlify.app) is a content-generation tool operated by MyPetStore (mypetstore.shop) for its own marketing. It generates advertising copy and publishes content about MyPetStore products to MyPetStore's own accounts.

Information we collect

  • Visitors: the app does not require accounts, does not use analytics or advertising trackers, and does not collect personal information from visitors. Product details typed into the ad generator are sent to an AI service (Anthropic's Claude API) solely to generate ad copy and are not stored by the app.
  • Connected services: the app stores OAuth access tokens for third-party platforms (such as Pinterest and Shopify) that the store owner has explicitly connected. These tokens are used only to publish MyPetStore's own content to MyPetStore's own accounts, and are stored securely in the app's hosting infrastructure (Netlify).

How we use information

Data accessed through connected platforms (for example, reading MyPetStore's product catalog or creating pins on MyPetStore's Pinterest boards) is used exclusively to generate and publish marketing content for MyPetStore. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties, and we do not access data belonging to other users of those platforms.

Data retention and deletion

Generated content bundles are retained for display in the app's content calendar. OAuth tokens are retained until the store owner disconnects the integration, at which point they are deleted. To request deletion of any data, contact us at the email below.
